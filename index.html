HOME

Land Campaign is a public interest organisation dedicated to protecting land rights, promoting social justice, and securing the rightful land ownership of oppressed and marginalized communities.

The organisation works through research, legal advocacy, public awareness, and democratic public participation to highlight land rights issues and seek justice for historically disadvantaged communities.

1. LAND CAMPAIGN – MANIFESTO

Land Campaign is a public interest organisation established to protect land rights, social justice, and the fundamental rights of oppressed communities.

The primary objectives of this organisation are:

  • To reclaim, protect, and secure the land rights of oppressed and marginalized communities.
  • To reclaim and safeguard Depressed Classes Conditional Lands (Panchami Lands).
  • To intervene in illegal land occupation cases.
  • To expose unlawful land grabbing.
  • To secure justice for affected communities.
  • To create legal awareness.
  • To advocate land reforms.

Land Campaign functions as a voluntary public-interest organisation committed to equality and land rights.

2. ORGANISATION VISION

Vision: To build a just society where land rights are equally accessible to all people.

Mission:

  • To undertake legal and social actions against violations of land rights.
  • To expose injustices and irregularities related to land ownership.
  • To safeguard rightful ownership.
