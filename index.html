About Land Campaign

Land Campaign is a public interest organisation dedicated to protecting land rights, promoting social justice, and securing rightful ownership for marginalized communities.

Manifesto

  • Protect Panchami Lands
  • Stop illegal land grabbing
  • Secure justice for affected communities
  • Create legal awareness
  • Advocate land reforms

Vision

“Land is dignity, identity, and justice.”

To build a just society where land rights are protected for all.

Key Activities

  • Research land violations
  • Legal advocacy & PIL
  • Public awareness campaigns
  • Community mobilisation
