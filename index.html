Land LAND IS NOT JUST PROPERTY. IT IS DIGNITY, IDENTITY AND JUSTICE

Introduction

We admire the great man J. H. A. Tremenheere who recommended conditional land grants to the depressed classes of India, and Queen Victoria who, on the basis of that report, passed the D.C. Land Allotment Bill in the Parliament of the United Kingdom in 1891.

About the Land Campaign

This campaign is led by social activists with over three decades of grassroots experience.

Our mission is to safeguard Panchami (D.C.) lands, prevent unlawful encroachments, and ensure that conditional lands are restored to their rightful owners.
