LAND CAMPAIGN

Land Rights • Social Justice • Equality

Land Campaign is a non governmental organisation dedicated to protecting land rights, promoting social justice, and securing the rightful land ownership of oppressed and marginalized communities.

The organisation works through research, legal advocacy, public awareness, and democratic public participation to highlight land rights issues and seek justice for historically disadvantaged communities.

📜 Manifesto

  • Restore Panchami lands
  • Protect marginalized communities
  • Stop illegal land grabbing
  • Expose corruption
  • Create legal awareness

🎯 Vision

To build a just society where land rights are equally accessible to all.

🚀 Mission

  • Legal action against violations
  • Expose injustice
  • Protect rightful ownership

⚖ Core Principles

Social Justice • Equality • Transparency • Accountability • Participation
