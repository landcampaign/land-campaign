Government Orders (Primary Evidence)

Note: These are original archival records from the Madras Record Office (1892).

Key Legal Points

  • G.O. No. 1010 (1892) legally assigned land to the Depressed Classes for their upliftment.
  • These lands are conditional assignments and cannot be sold, transferred, or alienated to others.
  • Any transfer of such lands to non-Depressed Classes is illegal and void under the original conditions.
  • The Government retains authority to restore such lands to the rightful beneficiaries.
  • This order forms the legal foundation of Panchami land rights in Tamil Nadu.
