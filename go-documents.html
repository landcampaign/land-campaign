Government Orders (Primary Evidence)

Note: These are original archival records from the Madras Record Office (1892), forming the legal foundation of Panchami land assignment.

G.O. No. 1010 (1892)

This Government Order establishes the assignment of land to the Depressed Classes for their social and economic upliftment. These lands are conditionally assigned and cannot be legally transferred.

G.O. No. 1010-A (1892)

This supplementary Government Order provides the detailed administrative and legal framework governing the Panchami land system and its protection.
