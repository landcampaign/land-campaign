Evidence

📌 Note

These are original archival records from the Madras Record Office (1892), forming the legal foundation of Panchami land rights.

⚖️ Key Legal Points

  • G.O. No. 1010 (1892) assigned land to Depressed Classes.
  • These lands cannot be sold or transferred.
  • Illegal transfers are void.
  • Government can restore lands to rightful owners.
  • This forms the foundation of Panchami land rights.
