Our Work

⚖️ Land Rights Protection

We actively work to protect the land rights of marginalized and oppressed communities, ensuring legal ownership and preventing unlawful dispossession.

🌾 Panchami Land Recovery

Our campaign focuses on identifying, We actively identify, document, and pursue recovery of Panchami lands that have been illegally occupied or transferred, ensuring restoration to rightful beneficiaries.

📜 Legal Action & PILs

We file Public Interest Litigations (PILs), complaints, and represent affected people in legal forums to secure justice.

📢 Public Awareness

Through campaigns, meetings, and education programs, we spread awareness about land rights and legal protections.

Our Approach

  1. Identification of Panchami lands
  2. Documentation of violations
  3. Legal action and complaints
  4. Public awareness and mobilisation
  5. Restoration and monitoring

🤝 Community Mobilisation

We organize peaceful movements and build collective strength among people to fight against land injustice.
