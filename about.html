🏛️ LAND CAMPAIGN

Land Campaign is a public interest organisation dedicated to protecting land rights, promoting social justice, and securing rightful land ownership for marginalized communities.

📜 Manifesto

  • Protect land rights of oppressed communities
  • Restore Panchami lands
  • Stop illegal land grabbing
  • Expose corruption
  • Create legal awareness

🎯 Vision

To build a just society where land rights are equal for all.

🚀 Mission

  • Legal action against land violations
  • Expose injustice
  • Protect rightful ownership

⚖️ Core Principles

Social Justice • Equality • Transparency • Accountability • Participation
