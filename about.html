1. Manifesto

Land Campaign is a non-governmental organization established to protect land rights, social justice, and the fundamental rights of oppressed communities.

  • Protect land rights
  • Safeguard Panchami Lands
  • Stop illegal land grabbing
  • Expose corruption
  • Create legal awareness
  • Advocate land reforms

2. Vision & Mission

Vision: Equal land access for all people.

  • Legal action against land violations
  • Expose injustice in land ownership
  • Protect marginalized communities

3. Core Principles

Social Justice • Equality • Transparency • Accountability • People’s Participation
