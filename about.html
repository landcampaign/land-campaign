ABOUT

🏛 Land Campaign

Land Campaign is a non-governmental organisation dedicated to protecting land rights, promoting social justice, and securing rightful land ownership for marginalized communities.

📜 Manifesto

  • Protect the land rights of oppressed communities
  • Restore Panchami lands to rightful beneficiaries
  • Act against illegal land grabbing
  • Expose breaches of legal and administrative duty
  • Promote legal awareness among affected communities

🎯 Vision

To build a just society where land rights are protected and upheld.

🚀 Mission

To restore lost land rights in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

⚖ Core Principles

Social Justice · Equality · Transparency · Accountability · Participation
