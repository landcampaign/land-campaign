🏛 About Land Campaign

Land Campaign is a people-led initiative dedicated to protecting land rights, promoting social justice, and securing lawful land ownership for marginalized communities.

🎯 Our Objectives

  • Protect the land rights of oppressed communities
  • Restore Panchami lands to rightful beneficiaries
  • Act against illegal land grabbing
  • Expose breaches of legal and administrative duty
  • Promote legal awareness among affected communities

🎯 Vision

To build a just and equitable society where land rights are protected and enforced.

🚀 Mission

To secure, protect, and restore land rights in accordance with law.

⚖ Core Principles

Social Justice · Equality · Transparency · Accountability · Participation
