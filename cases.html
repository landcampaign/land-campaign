Legal Cases & Actions

⚖️ Panchami Land Violations

Identification and documentation of Panchami lands that have been illegally occupied, transferred, or misused in violation of legal provisions.

🚫 Illegal Land Grabbing

Monitoring and exposing cases where powerful individuals or entities have unlawfully taken control of lands belonging to marginalized communities.

🏛️ Public Interest Litigations (PILs)

Filing PILs in courts to challenge illegal land transactions and to ensure justice and restoration of rightful ownership.

📄 Government Complaints

Submitting formal complaints and representations to authorities seeking legal action against land violations and corruption.

📰 Case Documentation & Reports

Preparing detailed reports, evidence documentation, and case records to support legal proceedings and public awareness.
