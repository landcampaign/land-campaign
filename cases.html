Legal Cases & Actions

⚖️ Legal Violations & Remedies

Numerous instances of unlawful encroachments, illegal transfers, and unauthorized occupation of Panchami lands have been observed.

Such actions are in direct violation of the conditions of assignment and are liable to be declared null and void.

⚖️ Panchami Land Violations

Documented cases of illegal occupation, unlawful transfer, and misuses of Panchami Land in violation of statutory protections. These cases from the basis for legal action and recovery.

🏛️ Public Interest Litigations (PILs)

Filing PILs in courts to challenge illegal land transactions and to ensure justice and restoration of rightful ownership.

📄 Government Complaints

Submitting formal complaints and representations to authorities seeking legal action against land violations and corruption.

Relief Sought / Demands

  • Immediate identification and retrieval of encroached Panchami lands
  • Strict enforcement of Government Orders
  • Legal action against violators
  • Restoration to rightful beneficiaries
